Meet Your Coaches

Our names are Henrik and Megan, husband and wife with two amazing kids. We met while competing in BMX racing, have lived in Sweden for the past 8 years, and have now relocated to Florida.

We are both strength coaches as well as licensed massage therapists who believe that recovery is just as important as training. That’s why we combine strength training with massage therapy for a complete, whole-body approach to fitness and performance.

Henrik Thorsen

Performance & Strength Specialist

Henrik is a highly dedicated and accomplished fitness professional with over 20 years of hands-on training experience in performance coaching, strength development, and athletic conditioning. As a former professional BMX racer, he brings elite athletic insight, discipline, and a results-driven mindset to every client he works with.

Professional Experience

  • Holistic approach integrating mental resilience and evidence-based nutrition

Coaching Philosophy

Henrik’s coaching style is built on personalization, science, and purpose. He believes every client deserves an approach tailored to their unique goals. By combining technical expertise with real-world athletic experience, he empowers clients to push past limitations and unlock peak performance.

Megan Thorsen

Strength Coach & Licensed Massage Therapist

Megan combines strength coaching with therapeutic bodywork to help clients move better, feel stronger, and recover faster. With a background in BMX racing and years of hands-on coaching experience, she understands both performance and longevity.

Specialties

  • Strength training for women
  • Pre- and postnatal fitness
  • Swedish classic massage
  • Sports massage & recovery therapy
  • Movement quality and injury prevention

Coaching Philosophy

Megan believes strength is for everyone and that training should enhance your life—not exhaust it. By combining intelligent programming with restorative massage techniques, she helps clients build confidence, resilience, and sustainable progress.
