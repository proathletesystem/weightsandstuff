Frequently asked questions

Do I need experience to start?

Nope. We meet you where you are and progress at your pace.

Is training during pregnancy safe?

With appropriate modifications and monitoring, strength training is beneficial for most people during pregnancy. We’ll coordinate with your care provider as needed.

Do you take insurance?

Training is private‑pay; massage receipts are available for reimbursement where applicable.
